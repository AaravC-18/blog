Places Must Visit

adventure cove

I had visited in june 2022

Adventutre Cove is simply the best waterpark in Singapore! It is located in Sentosa Cove. This place is a must visit as it is so much fun! This is because of it's breath-taking water slides and spectacular scenary. There are 3 water slides that all have something unique about them, one is extremely fast, one has an amazing view and one makes you go dizzy. On top of that there is a wave pool that is so much fun. There is also a lazy river that surrounds the waterpark! It is a great experience and I highly recommend visiting here!
